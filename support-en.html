Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I use the app?

A: After launching the app, first register your child's name and birthday. Then, tap on your child's card to add events (milestones). You can choose from three types: age-based, date-based, or annual events.

Q: Tell me about event types

A: There are three types:
Age-based: Set milestones like "until 18 years old"
Date-based: Set specific dates like "until graduation"
Annual Events: Set recurring events like "until birthday"

Q: How are weekends and holidays calculated?

A: Weekends are automatically calculated as Saturdays and Sundays. Holidays are calculated using the holiday calendar of the country selected in settings. Holidays are counted excluding those that overlap with weekends.

Q: Tell me about premium features

A: The basic version allows one event per child. The premium version allows unlimited event registration. You can purchase it from the App Store.

Q: Can I change card colors?

A: Yes, you can set card colors for each child from the settings screen. You can manage multiple children's information with color-coded cards.

Q: Can I change the language?

A: Yes, you can change the language from the settings screen to Japanese, English, Chinese, Korean, or Spanish.

Q: Where is data stored?

A: All data is stored locally on your device. It is not transmitted to external servers.

Troubleshooting

  • App won't launch: Try restarting your device and ensure you have the latest version of iOS
  • Countdown not displaying: Check if the event date is set correctly. Past dates will not be displayed
  • Weekend/holiday calculation incorrect: Check if the correct country is selected in settings
  • Premium features not working: Check if the purchase is completed in the App Store. Try restarting the app after purchase
  • App crashes: Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the problem persists, restart your device
