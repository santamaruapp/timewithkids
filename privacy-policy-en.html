Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2024

1. Introduction

Time with Kids (the "App") is an application that displays remaining time until your child's growth milestones. We respect our users' privacy and are committed to protecting personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Recorded in the App

  • Children's names and birthdays
  • Event (milestone) information you set
  • Personal settings such as card colors
  • App settings such as language preferences
  • App usage statistics (stored locally only)

2.2 Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personally identifiable information (address, phone number, email address, etc.)
  • Location information
  • Contact information
  • Photos or videos
  • Third-party service integration information

3. Data Storage and Management

All data recorded in this app is stored locally on your device. Data is not transmitted to external servers.

3.1 Data Backup

Data may be stored in cloud services such as iCloud only when users explicitly use the backup feature. In this case, data is encrypted before storage.

3.2 Data Synchronization

This app does not provide data synchronization across multiple devices. Data is managed independently on each device.

4. Information Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share users' personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When required by law
  • When users have given explicit consent
  • Anonymized statistical data for app improvement (only information that cannot identify individuals)

5. Data Deletion

When the app is deleted, all data stored on the device will be deleted. You can also manually delete data from the app settings.

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If children under 13 use the app, please use it under parental supervision.

7. Premium Features

This app includes premium features that can be purchased through the App Store. Purchase information is managed by Apple's system and we do not have direct access to it.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as necessary. If there are significant changes, we will notify you within the app.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
